Alpha Medical

AI Clinical Decision Support — powered by multi-LLM consensus

Overview

Alpha Medical is a clinical decision support system that combines multiple artificial intelligence models with curated medical knowledge bases to deliver high-confidence diagnostic insights. Designed for healthcare professionals, medical device developers, and clinical researchers who need reliable, evidence-based guidance.

Unlike traditional single-model AI tools, Alpha Medical uses a proprietary consensus engine that queries multiple independent AI models simultaneously and synthesizes their responses, dramatically reducing the risk of AI hallucinations.

Key Features

Consensus Engine

3-LLM querying with transparent agreement levels (High, Medium, Low) so you always know how much to trust each response.

AI Medical Consultation

Real-time conversational interface with a medical diagnostics agent. Multi-turn conversations with full citations and rich formatting.

FDA Pathway Advisor

Guided 4-step wizard recommending the appropriate FDA regulatory pathway (510(k), De Novo, PMA, or Exempt) for your device.

Benchmark Tool

Submit diagnostic claims and compare against FDA standards, peer-reviewed literature, and published accuracy metrics.

Knowledge Base

Over 15,000 curated medical embeddings spanning FDA device data, clinical guidelines, research literature, and diagnostic standards.

Expert Feedback Loop

Expert-weighted clinical corrections (up to 15x multiplier) are ingested as lessons that directly improve future AI responses.

How It Works

When Consensus Mode is enabled, three independent large language models evaluate your clinical question simultaneously. Each model generates its response independently, and the system synthesizes the results:

  • High Agreement — All three models concur. Highest confidence.
  • Medium Agreement — Two of three agree. Review recommended.
  • Low Agreement — Models disagree. Uncertainty is flagged transparently.

Every response is grounded in a curated knowledge base using retrieval-augmented generation, ensuring answers are anchored in evidence rather than generated purely from training data.

  • Android: APK available for beta testers
