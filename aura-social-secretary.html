Aura Social Secretary

Your AI-powered social intelligence assistant — relationships, energy, and scheduling handled

What Is Aura Social Secretary?

Aura Social Secretary is an intelligent assistant that helps you navigate your social world with confidence. It quietly learns about the people in your life, remembers the details you would otherwise forget, and prepares you before every interaction so you always show up as your best self.

Beyond memory, Aura monitors your social energy levels to protect you from burnout, handles scheduling through AI agent-to-agent coordination (so you never have to send another "does Tuesday work?" text), and provides real-time insights into your relationship dynamics.

Privacy-First Architecture: Aura processes everything on your device. Your relationship data, conversation notes, and social patterns never leave your phone. There are no cloud servers storing your personal social graph.

Key Features

Memory Whispers

Before a meeting or event, Aura sends you a discreet notification with key details: the last time you spoke, what you discussed, their interests, and suggested conversation starters.

Social Battery

Tracks your social energy based on interaction frequency, event types, and your self-reported mood. When your battery is low, Aura suggests lighter commitments or free time.

Smart Scheduling

Agent-to-agent coordination means Aura talks to the other person's scheduling assistant (or calendar) to find mutual availability. No more back-and-forth text chains.

Vibes

Real-time social dynamics dashboard showing relationship health scores, interaction frequency, and sentiment trends. See at a glance which relationships need attention.

Synaptic Contacts

Rich contact profiles that go far beyond phone number and email. Store personality notes, communication preferences, shared memories, gift ideas, and important dates.

Event Prep

Before group events, get a briefing on all attendees: who is coming, your history with each person, potential conversation topics, and any sensitivities to be aware of.

App Screens & How You Interact

Home Screen

Social Dashboard

The Home screen gives you an overview of your social world at a glance. It is designed to be checked quickly, like a weather app for your social life.

  • Social Battery Gauge: A visual indicator showing your current energy level. Ranges from fully charged (green) to depleted (red), based on recent activity and your personal patterns.
  • Upcoming Events: A timeline of your next social commitments with attendee previews. Tap any event to see your Memory Whisper briefing.
  • Relationship Alerts: Gentle nudges when you have not connected with someone important in a while, or when a birthday or anniversary is approaching.
  • Quick Actions: Schedule a meetup, add a contact note, or log a social interaction in seconds.
Memory Whispers

Pre-Interaction Briefings

This is what makes Aura unique. Before any meeting, coffee, dinner, or event, you receive an automatically generated briefing about the people you are about to see.

  • Last Interaction Summary: When you last spoke, what you discussed, and any follow-ups you mentioned ("I'll send you that article").
  • Conversation Starters: AI-generated openers based on their recent interests, current events in their life, or shared experiences.
  • Sensitivities: Reminders about topics to avoid or approach carefully (recent job loss, health issue, breakup).
  • Gift & Interest Notes: Things they have mentioned wanting, hobbies they enjoy, and preferences you have noted over time.
Vibes Dashboard

Relationship Health at a Glance

The Vibes screen visualizes the health and dynamics of your social network using intuitive charts and color-coded indicators.

  • Relationship Health Scores: Each contact gets a health score based on interaction frequency, sentiment, and reciprocity. Green means thriving; amber means it needs attention.
  • Interaction Timeline: A visual history showing when and how often you connect with each person. Spot patterns and seasonal changes.
  • Inner Circle vs. Outer Circle: A concentric view of your social network, with your closest relationships at the center and acquaintances on the outer rings.
Scheduling

Agent-to-Agent Coordination

Tell Aura you want to meet someone, and it handles the rest. No more "when are you free?" text chains.

  • Initiate a Meetup: Say "Schedule coffee with Sarah this week" and Aura checks your calendar, identifies open slots, and coordinates with Sarah's availability.
  • Mutual Availability: Aura communicates with the other person's scheduling agent or calendar to find overlapping free time without revealing your full schedule.
  • Confirmation & Reminders: Once a time is agreed, both parties receive a calendar event and a reminder with the Memory Whisper briefing before the meeting.

How It Works

1

Build Your Social Graph

Import contacts or add people manually. For each person, add notes about your relationship, shared interests, and important details. Aura learns more with every interaction you log.

2

Get Briefed Before Interactions

Before meetings and events, Aura sends Memory Whispers with everything you need to know. You walk in prepared, remembering details that strengthen your relationships.

3

Monitor Your Energy

The Social Battery tracks your engagement levels. When you are running low, Aura suggests scaling back or taking social downtime before you hit burnout.

4

Let Aura Handle Scheduling

Instead of texting back and forth, tell Aura who you want to see and when. It coordinates the logistics while you focus on the relationship itself.

Who Is Aura For?

  • Busy professionals who struggle to maintain personal relationships alongside demanding careers
  • People who feel socially drained and need help managing their energy and commitments
  • Anyone who forgets names, details, or follow-ups and wants AI-assisted social memory
  • Networking-heavy roles (sales, consulting, leadership) where remembering personal details creates real professional advantage

Privacy & Data

Your social life is deeply personal. That is why Aura processes 100% of your data on-device. Your relationship notes, social graph, interaction history, and energy data never leave your phone. There are no cloud servers, no data syncing, and no third-party access.
