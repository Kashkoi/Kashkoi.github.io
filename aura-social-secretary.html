Aura Social Secretary

Your AI-Powered Social Intelligence Assistant — privacy-first, on-device

Overview

Aura is your personal AI social secretary — an intelligent assistant that lives on your phone and helps you navigate your social world with confidence. It remembers everything about your relationships and gently reminds you of the right things to say at the right time.

Unlike other social apps, Aura doesn't harvest your data or send it to the cloud. Everything — your contacts, conversations, and calendar — is processed entirely on your device.

Key Features

Daily Briefing

A personalized summary of your social world: priority items, reconnect suggestions, calendar events with social context.

Memory Whispers

AI-generated conversation prep before meetings. Recent context, good topics, sensitive subjects, and conversation starters.

Social Battery

Tracks your social energy and activates a "digital firewall" when you're running low, suppressing non-urgent notifications.

Smart Scheduling

AI agents negotiate meeting times on your behalf without sharing calendar details. Privacy-preserving coordination.

Vibes

Broadcast your mood to close friends through beautiful abstract visuals — no words needed.

Synaptic Contacts

Brain-inspired contact strength tracking. Connections strengthen with use and naturally fade without interaction.

How It Works

Aura uses a system inspired by how the brain works — connections that are used often grow stronger, while unused ones gradually fade. Each contact has a "synaptic weight" from 0% to 100%, learned automatically from your interactions.

  • Every call, text, or meeting strengthens the connection
  • Long periods of no contact cause the weight to gradually decay
  • Contacts below 40% that haven't been reached in 2+ weeks are marked as "Fading"
Privacy by Design: On-device processing. Hashed identifiers (SHA-256). Privacy-preserving scheduling shares only free/busy slots. No advertising, no tracking, no data brokers. Your social intelligence is yours alone.
