Concierge

AI Email Assistant — classify, prioritize, and conquer your inbox

Overview

Concierge is your AI-powered email assistant that uses Claude AI to intelligently classify, prioritize, and manage your inbox. It learns your preferences over time to automate routine email tasks, surface what matters, and keep your inbox clean.

AI Email Classification

Every email is automatically classified into one of six categories with color-coded badges and confidence scores:

CategoryDescription
UrgentTime-sensitive emails with deadlines within 48 hours, critical requests, or outages
Follow-upEmails needing your response — meeting invites, action items, pending conversations
PersonalMessages from friends, family, and personal contacts
RecordsReceipts, confirmations, bank statements, tax forms, and documents to keep
OffersDeals, coupons, promotions, and newsletters with detected expiry dates
JunkSpam, phishing attempts, scams, and unsolicited marketing

Key Features

AI Draft Reply

Contextual AI-generated reply suggestions you can edit and send with one tap.

Calendar Extraction

Detects meetings in emails and extracts event details directly to your Google Calendar.

Voice Memos

Audio summaries of long emails you can listen to on the go. Choose voice and speed.

Drive Archival

One-tap attachment saving to Google Drive, organized by category.

Bulk Cleanup

Multi-select, bulk trash, bulk unsubscribe, and one-tap junk purge.

Smart Automation

Configurable auto-scan (hourly/daily), auto-purge expired offers, and rule-based actions.

Privacy Mode

Concierge includes a local-only Privacy Mode that uses keyword matching instead of cloud AI. Lower accuracy, but no data leaves your device. You control how your email is processed.
