How to request that your Alpha Inception account and associated data be deleted.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data using any of the following methods:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Scroll down to Account
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account.

privacy@alphainception.com

What Gets Deleted

Data TypeActionTimeframe
Account credentials (name, email)Permanently deletedWithin 30 days
User-generated content (queries, feedback, drafts)Permanently deletedWithin 30 days
Health profile dataPermanently deletedWithin 30 days
Subscription statusCancelled and deletedWithin 30 days
On-device data (local storage)Deleted when you uninstall the appImmediate
Anonymized analyticsRetained (cannot identify you)N/A
Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, it will be cancelled but you may need to also cancel it through the App Store or Google Play to stop future billing.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been fully deleted.
