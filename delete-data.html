Delete Your Data

Request deletion of some or all of your data without deleting your account.

You don't have to delete your account. You can request deletion of specific data while keeping your account active.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: In-App

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Tap "Privacy" or "Manage Data"
  3. Select the types of data you want deleted
  4. Tap "Delete Selected Data" and confirm

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to the address below with the subject "Data Deletion Request". Specify which data you want deleted.

privacy@alphainception.com

Data Retention After Deletion

  • Requested data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained.
  • Data required by law (e.g., transaction records) may be retained for the legally required period.

On-Device Data

Some of our apps (Aura Social Secretary, Concierge in Privacy Mode) store data exclusively on your device. This data is never on our servers. To delete it:

  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in the app's Settings
  • Or simply uninstall the app — all local data is removed

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the requested data has been deleted.
