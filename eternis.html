Memory & Legacy

Eternis

Living Memory Platform — AI-guided personal and family memory management with time capsules, digital self, genealogy, and cognitive health integration

What Is Eternis?

Eternis is a living memory platform that captures, preserves, organizes, and intelligently processes your lifetime memories. It goes far beyond a journal or photo album — Eternis uses AI to actively elicit your stories through adaptive questioning, connects memories across family generations through a collaborative genealogy vault, and creates a digital representation of yourself that your loved ones can interact with.

At its core, Eternis is built around the idea that memories are too important to leave to chance. Most of us lose the details of our lives simply because no one asked the right questions at the right time. Eternis asks those questions, preserves your answers in multiple media formats, and weaves them into a coherent narrative that grows richer over time.

Cognitive Intelligence Integration: Eternis feeds journal entries and memory text into the NLCS (Neuro-Linguistic Cognitive Sentinel) ecosystem, which analyzes 28 linguistic features to track cognitive trajectory over time. This means your everyday journaling becomes a passive cognitive health monitoring tool — detecting subtle linguistic shifts that may indicate early cognitive changes, years before clinical symptoms appear.

The 6 Pillars

Eternis is organized around six interconnected pillars, each addressing a different dimension of memory and legacy.

1

Personal Memory Agent

AI-guided journaling that goes beyond a blank page. The Memory Agent uses Thompson Sampling to learn which types of questions elicit your richest memories and adapts its prompts over time.

  • Text, voice memo, photo, and video memory capture
  • Adaptive AI questioning that learns what resonates with you
  • Automatic mood tracking and life-stage tagging
  • Location-based memory triggers and annotations
  • Voice-to-text transcription via Whisper for spoken memories
  • Daily prompt scheduling to build a consistent journaling habit
2

Family Vault & Genealogy

A collaborative space where your entire family contributes memories, builds a living family tree, and preserves health history across generations.

  • Interactive family tree with GEDCOM import/export
  • Invite family members to contribute their own memories
  • Collaborative memory collection tied to family events
  • Family health history tracking across generations
  • Shared photo and video albums with AI-powered organization
  • Permission controls for who can view or contribute
3

Cooperative Challenge Game

A multiplayer memory game that turns memory sharing into an engaging group activity. Family members and friends collaborate on themed challenges to unlock shared memories.

  • Themed memory challenges with multiple modalities
  • Encrypted contributions that only unlock when everyone participates
  • Unlock tokens for accessing shared memory collections
  • Leaderboards and streak tracking for engagement
  • Cross-family challenges for reunions and holidays
4

Time Capsule System

Create sealed memory containers with sophisticated release mechanisms. Messages, photos, and videos are encrypted and locked until the conditions you set are met.

  • Temporal Release: Opens on a specific future date
  • Event-Based: Triggered by a milestone (graduation, wedding, birthday)
  • Mortality Trigger: Dead-man's-switch that releases if you stop checking in
  • Consensus-Based: Requires multiple designated people to agree to open
  • AES-256-GCM encryption with Shamir secret sharing
  • Multiple recipients with individual access controls
5

Digital Self / Virtual You

An AI personality model trained from your journal entries that captures your voice, perspectives, and personality. Your loved ones can have conversations with your digital self.

  • AI personality model built from your journaling history
  • Voice synthesis that sounds like you
  • Chat interface for family to interact with your digital self
  • Personality weight tuning and activation controls
  • Opt-in only — you choose when and if to activate
  • Graceful handling of questions outside your memory scope
6

Health Integration

Automatically extracts health observations from your memories using NLP, creating a longitudinal health record that bridges personal narrative with clinical data.

  • NLP extraction of health conditions, symptoms, and observations from journal text
  • SNOMED/ICD-11 coded health observations
  • FHIR R4 export for sharing with healthcare providers
  • Family health history aggregation across generations
  • NLCS cognitive trajectory scoring from linguistic patterns
  • 28 linguistic feature analysis for early cognitive monitoring

Key Features

Adaptive AI Prompts

Thompson Sampling algorithm learns which types of questions elicit your best memories. The system gets better at asking the right questions the more you use it, ensuring meaningful capture rather than generic journaling.

Multi-Media Capture

Memories are not just text. Capture voice memos transcribed by Whisper AI, photos with location and context tags, videos, and documents. Every format is searchable and interconnected.

Military-Grade Encryption

Time capsules use AES-256-GCM encryption with Shamir secret sharing for consensus-based releases. Your most private memories are protected with the same cryptographic standards used by intelligence agencies.

Cognitive Health Monitoring

NLCS integration analyzes 28 linguistic features in your journal entries to track cognitive trajectory. Subtle changes in word diversity, sentence complexity, and semantic coherence are flagged before clinical symptoms appear.

FHIR Health Export

Health observations extracted from your memories are coded in SNOMED/ICD-11 and exportable as FHIR R4 Observations with LOINC coding. Share a longitudinal health narrative with your doctor, not just lab results.

Dead-Man's Switch

The mortality trigger checks whether you are still active. If you stop responding to periodic check-ins, designated time capsules and digital self access are released to your chosen recipients according to your instructions.

Narrative Synthesis

AI-powered post-event montage and book generation. After a trip, holiday, or life milestone, Eternis can compile your memories into a cohesive narrative with photos, quotes, and timeline visualizations.

Ecosystem Integration

Eternis feeds into the broader Alpha Inception health ecosystem. Journal data flows to NLCS for cognitive monitoring, health observations connect with Synapta therapy records, and family health history enriches NeuroGuard baselines.

App Screens & How You Interact

Memory Journal

Your Daily Memory Space

The journal is where most of your interaction with Eternis happens. It is designed to make memory capture effortless, whether you have thirty seconds or thirty minutes.

  • AI Prompt Card: Each day opens with a personalized prompt from the Memory Agent. Questions like "Tell me about a meal that changed how you think about food" or "What did your childhood home smell like?" are chosen by Thompson Sampling based on what has worked best for you.
  • Quick Capture: Tap the microphone to record a voice memo, the camera for a photo, or just start typing. Every entry is automatically tagged with date, location, mood, and life stage.
  • Memory Timeline: Scroll through your memories chronologically or search by topic, person, location, or emotion. The AI surfaces connections between memories you may not have noticed.
  • Mood Tracker: Each entry includes an optional mood indicator. Over time, this builds a longitudinal emotional map that correlates with life events and journal themes.
  • Cognitive Pulse: A subtle indicator showing your recent NLCS cognitive trajectory score. This is derived entirely from your natural writing — no tests or assessments required.
Family Vault

Collaborative Family Memory

The Family Vault turns memory preservation into a shared family activity. Multiple generations contribute memories that are woven into a living family narrative.

  • Family Tree: An interactive visual tree where each person is a node. Tap any family member to see their contributed memories, health history, and relationship connections.
  • Invite System: Send invitations to family members to join the vault. Each person controls what they share and with whom — no one can see your private memories unless you explicitly share them.
  • Shared Albums: Create photo and video collections around family events — holidays, reunions, milestones. Multiple family members can contribute to the same album.
  • Health History: A dedicated view that aggregates health conditions across the family tree. Useful for understanding genetic predispositions and sharing with healthcare providers.
  • GEDCOM Import: Already have genealogy data? Import GEDCOM files to bootstrap your family tree with existing research from Ancestry, FamilySearch, or other platforms.
Time Capsules

Sealed Messages for the Future

Create encrypted memory containers that are locked until specific conditions are met. This is Eternis at its most powerful — preserving your voice for moments you may not be present for.

  • Create a Capsule: Record a video message, write a letter, or compile photos. Choose your release mechanism: a specific date, a life event, a mortality trigger, or a consensus vote among designated people.
  • Recipient Management: Assign one or more recipients. Each recipient can have different access conditions — your daughter might receive a capsule on her wedding day while your best friend gets one on a specific anniversary.
  • Encryption Status: See the lock status of each capsule. Sealed capsules show their release conditions without revealing contents. Once released, recipients receive a notification and can view the contents.
  • Dead-Man's Switch: For mortality-triggered capsules, the system sends periodic check-in prompts. If you do not respond within your configured window, the release process begins with a grace period before final delivery.
  • Shamir Sharing: For consensus-based capsules, multiple key holders must agree to release. The system uses Shamir secret sharing so that no single person (including Alpha Inception) can access the contents alone.
Digital Self

Your AI Personality Model

The Digital Self is an AI model trained on your journal entries that captures your voice, opinions, humor, and worldview. Opt-in only — you decide if and when to activate it.

  • Training Progress: See how much data has been used to train your digital self. The model becomes more accurate as your journal grows. A minimum threshold of entries is required before activation is offered.
  • Chat Interface: Your family and designated contacts can have text and voice conversations with your digital self. The AI responds in your voice and perspective based on your journal history.
  • Voice Synthesis: The digital self can speak in a synthesized version of your voice, trained from your voice memo recordings. This creates a more personal and authentic interaction experience.
  • Activation Controls: Full control over when the digital self is active, who can access it, and what topics it can discuss. You can deactivate it at any time.
  • Scope Handling: When asked about topics outside your memory scope, the digital self responds honestly — "I don't have memories about that" rather than fabricating an answer.
Challenge Arena

Cooperative Memory Games

Turn memory sharing into a group activity. Family members and friends participate in themed challenges that unlock shared memory collections.

  • Active Challenges: See current challenges you have been invited to or created. Each challenge has a theme (childhood summers, family recipes, travel adventures) and a participation target.
  • Contribute: Submit your memory contribution to a challenge. Contributions are encrypted until all participants have submitted, preventing anyone from being influenced by others' responses.
  • Unlock and Reveal: Once all participants contribute, the collection unlocks and everyone can see each other's memories on the same theme. This often surfaces surprising connections and forgotten stories.
  • Leaderboard: Track participation streaks and challenge completions. Badges reward consistent engagement and multi-generational participation.
Health Dashboard

Memory-Derived Health Intelligence

Eternis quietly extracts health-relevant observations from your natural journaling, building a longitudinal health narrative without requiring you to fill out medical forms.

  • Extracted Observations: NLP identifies mentions of health conditions, symptoms, medications, and lifestyle factors from your journal entries. Each observation is coded in SNOMED/ICD-11 for clinical compatibility.
  • Cognitive Trajectory: NLCS integration tracks 28 linguistic features over time. Charts show trends in word diversity, semantic coherence, syntactic complexity, and other markers that correlate with cognitive health.
  • Family Health Map: Aggregated view of health conditions across your family tree. See patterns and predispositions that emerge when individual family members' health data is viewed together.
  • FHIR Export: One-tap export of your health observations as FHIR R4 resources. Share with your doctor to add a personal health narrative layer to your clinical records.
  • Alert Levels: The NLCS system flags Watch, Alert, and Critical levels if linguistic patterns suggest cognitive changes. These are informational and always paired with recommendations to consult a healthcare provider.

Privacy & Security

Your memories are among the most personal data imaginable, and Eternis treats them accordingly. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Time capsules use AES-256-GCM encryption with Shamir secret sharing — meaning that even Alpha Inception cannot access sealed capsules without the designated key holders' consent.

Authentication is handled through Supabase with support for email, Apple, and Google sign-in. Media files (photos, videos, voice memos) are stored in encrypted cloud storage with access controls tied to your account. Voice memos are transcribed by Whisper AI and the original audio is retained only if you choose to keep it.

Module activation is consent-based — each of the 6 pillars requires separate opt-in before any data collection begins. You can deactivate any module and request deletion of associated data at any time. NLCS cognitive analysis processes your text through secure Redis Streams with no data retention beyond the computed scores.
