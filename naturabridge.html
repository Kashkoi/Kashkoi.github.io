NaturaBridge

Evidence-based natural medicine — bridging Western, Ayurvedic & Traditional Chinese Medicine with AI consensus

What Is NaturaBridge?

NaturaBridge uses multi-agent AI consensus to research and recommend evidence-based natural alternatives alongside your existing prescriptions. It bridges three major medical traditions — Western medicine, Ayurveda, and Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) — to give you a holistic view of your wellness options.

Rather than replacing your doctor's advice, NaturaBridge acts as a research companion that surfaces peer-reviewed evidence for natural supplements, herbal remedies, and lifestyle modifications. Every recommendation includes a confidence score from multiple AI agents, interaction warnings with your current medications, and links to the underlying research.

Important Notice: NaturaBridge is an informational wellness tool. It does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before starting, stopping, or changing any medication or supplement regimen. Never discontinue prescribed medication based solely on app recommendations.

Key Features

Prescription Conversion

Enter a current medication and NaturaBridge researches natural alternatives with similar mechanisms of action. Results include evidence quality ratings and consensus confidence scores.

Constitutional Assessment

Complete a detailed quiz that combines Ayurvedic Dosha profiling (Vata, Pitta, Kapha) with TCM body constitution typing to create a personalized wellness profile.

Interaction Checker

Before adding any supplement, the interaction checker scans for conflicts with your current medications, other supplements, and known food interactions. Safety flags are color-coded.

Biometric Tracking

Connect Apple Health, Google Fit, or wearable devices to track how your body responds to changes. Monitor sleep, heart rate, energy levels, and other wellness indicators.

Multi-Tradition Research

For each condition or medication, see perspectives from Western evidence-based medicine, Ayurvedic tradition, and Traditional Chinese Medicine side by side, with citations for each.

AI Treatment Adjustments

As you track your biometrics and self-reported outcomes, the AI adjusts its recommendations over time, suggesting dosage modifications or alternative approaches based on your response data.

App Screens & How You Interact

Dashboard

Your Wellness Overview

The Dashboard shows your current supplement regimen, biometric trends, and any alerts or adjustments the AI is suggesting.

  • Active Regimen: A visual list of everything you are currently taking — both prescribed medications and natural supplements — with daily timing reminders.
  • Biometric Trends: Sparkline charts showing your key health indicators over the past week and month. Tap any chart to see detailed history.
  • AI Insights: Cards highlighting patterns the AI has noticed, such as improved sleep since starting magnesium or elevated heart rate that may warrant attention.
  • Safety Alerts: Any new interaction warnings or dosage concerns are displayed prominently at the top of the dashboard.
Prescription Converter

Research Natural Alternatives

The core research tool where you enter a medication and explore evidence-based natural options.

  • Medication Input: Type or scan a medication name. NaturaBridge identifies the drug class, mechanism of action, and common use cases.
  • Natural Alternatives: A ranked list of natural options with evidence quality ratings (Strong, Moderate, Preliminary) and consensus confidence from multiple AI agents.
  • Three-Tradition View: Toggle between Western, Ayurvedic, and TCM perspectives. Each tradition provides different remedies with different rationales, all with source citations.
  • Safety Check: Before you add anything to your regimen, the app runs an automatic interaction check against everything else you are taking.
Constitutional Quiz

Personalized Wellness Profiling

A comprehensive assessment that builds your unique health profile by combining two traditional medicine systems.

  • Ayurvedic Dosha Assessment: Answer questions about your body type, digestion, sleep patterns, emotional tendencies, and environmental preferences. Results identify your primary and secondary Doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha).
  • TCM Constitution Typing: Additional questions about temperature sensitivity, energy patterns, and organ system tendencies map you to one of nine TCM body constitutions.
  • Combined Profile: Your Dosha and TCM results are combined into a unified wellness profile that informs all future recommendations, from supplement choices to lifestyle advice.
  • Seasonal Adjustments: Your profile includes seasonal guidance — different recommendations for summer versus winter based on how your constitution responds to environmental changes.
Biometric Tracker

Monitor Your Response

Connect your health data sources and track how your body responds to changes in your wellness regimen.

  • Wearable Integration: Syncs with Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, Oura Ring, and other popular devices to pull in sleep, heart rate, HRV, steps, and other metrics automatically.
  • Self-Reported Data: Log subjective measures like energy level, mood, pain, digestion quality, and overall well-being on a simple daily scale.
  • Correlation Analysis: The AI looks for correlations between regimen changes and biometric shifts. For example, it might notice that your sleep quality improved two weeks after adding a specific supplement.
  • Progress Reports: Weekly and monthly summaries show how your key metrics have trended and what changes may be contributing to improvements or declines.
