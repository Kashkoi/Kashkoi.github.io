What Is NeuroGuard?

NeuroGuard is a comprehensive gamified health monitoring platform that turns routine neurological and physiological assessments into engaging mini-games and clinical scanning tools. Using your phone's built-in sensors — touchscreen, accelerometer, gyroscope, microphone, and camera — NeuroGuard captures clinically relevant biomarkers across motor function, cognitive performance, cardiovascular health, voice and speech patterns, gait and balance, oculomotor tracking, and metabolic indicators.

Beyond the games, NeuroGuard includes 12 dedicated clinical scanning tools for targeted assessments — from full-body anatomical scanning and ECG monitoring to non-invasive glucose measurement and fetal monitoring. An AI health chat companion provides context-aware wellness insights, while a four-way diagnostic consensus engine ensures every health finding is validated across independent AI models.

A three-tier emergency escalation system monitors for critical health events in real time, with automatic countdown timers, emergency contact notification, and 911 integration when needed. A digital twin model builds a personalized representation of your health profile for predictive diagnostics over time.

Built with Flutter 3.41 and Dart 3.11, NeuroGuard is currently available on iOS via TestFlight, with Android availability in progress.

ArbiterAI — 20-Step Clinical Intelligence Pipeline: NeuroGuard's diagnostic engine runs a 20-step pipeline including ICDL multi-model consensus, Immune Intelligence with clonal selection, Entropic Hallucination Detection, Traceboard auditing, Quorum Voting, Debate Engine with multi-model reasoning transparency, MACA (Multi-Agent Clinical Arbitration), Shadow Cabinet verification, Conformal Prediction with statistical guarantees, and Prediction Markets for probability-calibrated outputs. Each step adds a layer of validation before any health insight reaches the user.

Clinical Scanning Tools (12 Tools)

Beyond the games, NeuroGuard includes a suite of dedicated clinical scanning tools for targeted physiological assessments. These tools use your phone's sensors in focused, guided scanning sessions with calibration phases, real-time guidance interfaces, and confidence metrics for every measurement.

Body Scanner

Multi-target 2–5 minute full-body scan covering 50+ anatomical targets across head, chest, abdomen, extremities, and organs. Includes calibration phase, homing beacon guidance, and confidence metrics.

ECG Monitor

Electrocardiogram waveform capture and analysis. Records cardiac electrical activity and identifies rhythm patterns for cardiovascular health tracking.

Blood Pressure

Pulse transit time blood pressure estimation. Non-invasive systolic and diastolic measurement using sensor-derived pulse wave analysis.

Stroke Volume

Cardiac output assessment estimating stroke volume and ejection fraction for comprehensive cardiovascular performance monitoring.

Venous Refill

Venous insufficiency detection through refill time measurement. Assesses peripheral vascular health and circulatory function.

Stone Sweeper

Kidney stone detection via acoustic imaging simulation. Uses ultrasound-inspired techniques to identify potential renal calculi.

Elastography

Tissue elasticity assessment via vibration analysis. Measures tissue stiffness to screen for fibrosis and other structural changes.

Pulmonary

Lung sound analysis and pulmonary diagnostics. Captures respiratory sounds and analyzes breathing patterns for pulmonary health assessment.

Carotid Scan

Tri-modal carotid artery assessment. Evaluates carotid health through multiple measurement approaches for vascular risk screening.

Marangoni

Non-invasive glucose measurement via surface tension dynamics. Uses Marangoni effect principles to estimate blood glucose without needles.

Fetal Monitor

Pregnancy monitoring and fetal assessment. Tracks fetal movement, heart rate indicators, and maternal health parameters during pregnancy.

Breath Analysis

Respiratory biomarker analysis and breathalyzer functions. Identifies volatile organic compounds and respiratory patterns linked to health conditions.

Your Assessment Playground

The Games Hub presents all 16 game engines in a grid layout. Each game shows its name, target biomarker modality, estimated play time, adaptive difficulty level, and your last session score with baseline comparison.

  • Active Games: Games you have unlocked and calibrated appear at the top with full-color cards. Tap any game to jump into a session with instructions, calibration, gameplay, and results.

Emergency Escalation

Three-Tier Safety System

NeuroGuard continuously monitors for critical health events and provides an escalating response system designed to protect you when it matters most.

  • Tier 1 — Warning: Subtle notification when biomarkers show concerning deviations. Logged for trend analysis with a recommendation to re-test or consult a professional.
  • Tier 2 — Alert: Prominent alert with countdown timer and "I'M OK" acknowledgment button. If not acknowledged, the system escalates and notifies your designated emergency contacts.
  • Tier 3 — Critical: Full emergency protocol with emergency contact notification, Call 911 integration, and automatic location sharing. Activates when critical thresholds are breached or an alert goes unacknowledged.
  • FAST Stroke Protocol: Dedicated rapid Face-Arms-Speech-Time assessment that can be triggered manually or automatically, with structured alert escalation.
  • Escalation History Log: Complete record of all past alerts, responses, and outcomes for review by you or your healthcare provider.
Game Session

Playing a Game (Quick Tap Example)

Each game follows a consistent pattern: instructions, calibration, adaptive gameplay, and results with baseline comparison.

  • Instructions: A brief animation shows what to do. For Quick Tap: tap the targets as fast as you can. You play one round with your left hand, then one with your right.
  • Calibration: A short practice round establishes your session baseline. Difficulty adapts across levels 1–10 to keep you in the optimal challenge zone.
  • Gameplay: Targets appear at random positions. You tap as quickly and accurately as possible. Meanwhile, sensors capture reaction time, tap accuracy, touch pressure, and inter-tap interval for every touch.
  • Results: After both rounds, you see your score, accuracy percentage, streak status, and how you compare to your personal baseline. The data is processed through ArbiterAI and added to your biomarker history.

Privacy & Security

Health biomarker data demands the highest level of protection. NeuroGuard uses post-quantum cryptography (Kyber for key encapsulation, Dilithium for digital signatures) for data encryption, federated learning with Elastic Weight Consolidation (EWC) so your raw data never leaves your device for model training, and fully homomorphic encryption (CKKS scheme) for any cloud-based analysis. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and you can export or delete it at any time.

The privacy architecture ensures that even in the event of a future quantum computing breakthrough, your health data remains protected by cryptographic standards designed to withstand quantum attacks.
