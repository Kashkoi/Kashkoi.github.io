ProspectIQ DraftRoom

AI-powered NBA draft intelligence — scouting reports, mock drafts, comparisons & prediction leagues

What Is ProspectIQ DraftRoom?

ProspectIQ DraftRoom is the ultimate NBA draft companion for fans, analysts, and fantasy basketball players. It combines AI-generated scouting reports with interactive draft simulation tools, giving you deeper insight into every prospect than traditional coverage provides.

Whether you want to study individual players, simulate an entire draft with realistic trade logic, compare prospects head-to-head with statistical radar charts, or compete in prediction leagues with friends, DraftRoom puts professional-grade draft intelligence in your hands.

Key Features

Prospect Database

60+ prospects with AI-generated scouting reports covering strengths, weaknesses, NBA comparisons, projected role, and draft range. Updated as new data becomes available.

War Room Simulator

Run full mock drafts with AI-controlled teams that make realistic picks and trade proposals. Test different scenarios by trading up, down, or standing pat.

Head-to-Head Compare

Select any two prospects and see their stats overlaid on a radar chart. Compare scoring, playmaking, defense, athleticism, shooting, and intangibles side by side.

Fantasy Draft Advisor

Get round-by-round recommendations for your fantasy draft based on available players, your roster needs, and projected NBA roles and minutes.

Prediction Leagues

Create or join leagues where you predict draft order, trades, and player destinations. Track accuracy over time and compete on leaderboards with friends.

Trade Machine

Build hypothetical trade packages and see if the AI considers them realistic based on team needs, salary matching, and historical trade patterns.

App Screens & How You Interact

Prospect Database

Browse & Research Players

The Prospect Database is your starting point for draft research. Every player expected to be drafted is profiled with detailed AI-generated analysis.

  • Prospect List: Browse all 60+ prospects sorted by projected draft position. Filter by position (PG, SG, SF, PF, C), school, conference, or draft tier (Lottery, First Round, Second Round).
  • Scouting Report: Tap any prospect to see a comprehensive AI report covering their playing style, standout skills, areas for development, NBA player comparison, and projected role at the next level.
  • Statistical Profile: Key college stats presented in context — per-game averages, advanced metrics (PER, true shooting, assist ratio), and how they rank among their draft class peers.
  • Draft Range: A projected pick range (e.g., 8-14) with analysis of which teams are most likely landing spots based on roster fit and team needs.
War Room

Mock Draft Simulator

The War Room lets you experience draft night as a general manager. Control one team and watch AI-controlled teams make their picks around you.

  • Choose Your Team: Select any of the 30 NBA teams to control. The simulator pre-loads the team's actual draft picks, roster, and cap situation.
  • Live Draft Board: Watch picks unfold in real time. AI teams make selections based on their real-world needs, team-building philosophy, and prospect rankings.
  • Trade Proposals: At any point, propose a trade to move up or down in the draft. The AI evaluates your offer based on pick value, player value, and how it fits both teams' strategies.
  • Post-Draft Grades: After the simulation completes, every team receives a draft grade with analysis of how well they addressed their needs and the overall value they captured.
Head-to-Head

Prospect Comparison Tool

When you are torn between two prospects or want to understand what separates them, the Head-to-Head tool lays it out visually.

  • Select Two Prospects: Pick any two players from the database. Works best when comparing players at the same position or within the same draft tier.
  • Radar Chart: A visual overlay showing six key dimensions — Scoring, Playmaking, Defense, Athleticism, Shooting, and Intangibles — scaled relative to their draft class.
  • Stat Comparison Table: Side-by-side statistical breakdown with color highlighting to show who leads in each category.
  • AI Verdict: A brief AI-generated summary of who projects better in specific situations (win-now team vs. rebuilding team, high-usage role vs. complementary role).
Prediction League

Compete With Friends

The social side of DraftRoom. Make your predictions, see how they stack up against friends and the community, and track your accuracy over time.

  • Create a League: Set up a private league and invite friends via share link. Or join public leagues to compete with the broader DraftRoom community.
  • Make Predictions: Predict the full first-round order, individual pick selections, and potential trades before draft night. Lock in your predictions before the deadline.
  • Live Scoring: On draft night, watch your accuracy score update in real time as picks are announced. Points are awarded for correct picks, correct draft range, and correct team.
  • Leaderboards: See how you rank against other league members and the global community. Historical accuracy tracking lets you see if you are getting better each year.
