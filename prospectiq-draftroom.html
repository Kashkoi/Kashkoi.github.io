ProspectIQ DraftRoom

AI-Powered NBA Draft Intelligence — scouting, simulation & prediction

Overview

ProspectIQ DraftRoom is the ultimate NBA draft companion. Featuring AI-generated scouting reports, mock draft simulations, head-to-head prospect comparisons, prediction leagues, and fantasy draft advice — all powered by Claude AI.

Key Features

Prospect Database

60+ prospects with detailed profiles, stats, radar charts, skill grades across 6 categories, and AI scouting reports.

AI Scouting Reports

Strengths, weaknesses, historical player comparisons, best/worst case projections, and ideal team fit analysis.

War Room Simulator

Full mock draft sim with Full Control or Quick Sim modes. AI pick recommendations, trade proposals, and draft grades (A-F).

Head-to-Head Compare

Side-by-side prospect comparison with overlaid radar charts and AI narrative analysis of ceiling, floor, and edge.

Prediction Leagues

Create your 30-pick mock draft, compete in public or private leagues, and climb the global leaderboard.

Fantasy Draft Advisor

AI-powered pick recommendations tailored to your league size, scoring format, draft position, and rounds.
