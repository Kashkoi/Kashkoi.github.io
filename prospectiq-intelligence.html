ProspectIQ Intelligence

Advanced AI sports analytics and intelligence platform

Overview

ProspectIQ Intelligence is the analytics powerhouse behind the ProspectIQ ecosystem. While DraftRoom focuses on the NBA draft experience, Intelligence provides deep analytical capabilities including advanced statistical modeling, team-building optimization, trade impact analysis, and multi-season projection systems.

Built for front office analysts, serious fantasy players, and sports media professionals who need data-driven insights beyond basic box scores.

Key Features

Advanced Analytics

Deep statistical modeling beyond traditional box scores. Player impact metrics, lineup optimization, and predictive performance models powered by AI.

Trade Analyzer

Evaluate proposed trades with AI-powered impact analysis. Salary cap implications, roster fit scoring, and multi-season value projections.

Team Builder

AI-optimized roster construction tool. Build championship-caliber teams within salary constraints using multi-variable optimization.

Season Projections

Multi-season player and team projections using machine learning models trained on historical NBA data and current performance trends.

Scouting Network

Comprehensive prospect evaluation system with international player tracking, college performance analysis, and NBA readiness scoring.

Custom Reports

Generate detailed analytical reports for any player, team, or matchup. Export-ready formats for presentations and front office briefings.

How It Works

ProspectIQ Intelligence combines multiple data sources with AI analysis:

  • Historical NBA data spanning multiple decades provides the foundation for predictive models
  • Real-time performance tracking captures current season trends and momentum shifts
  • AI models evaluate player development trajectories and project future performance
  • Trade and roster optimization algorithms consider salary cap, team chemistry, and positional needs simultaneously
