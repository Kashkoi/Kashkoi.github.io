ProspectIQ Intelligence

Advanced AI sports analytics platform — player impact metrics, trade modeling, roster optimization, multi-season projections, and scouting intelligence

What Is ProspectIQ Intelligence?

ProspectIQ Intelligence is the analytics engine behind the ProspectIQ ecosystem. While DraftRoom focuses on the draft-day experience, Intelligence provides deep analytical capabilities for year-round decision-making. It combines historical NBA data spanning multiple decades with real-time performance tracking and machine learning models to deliver data-driven insights that go far beyond traditional box scores.

Built for front office analysts who evaluate trades at 2am, serious fantasy players who need an edge over their league, and sports media professionals who want data-backed narratives rather than hot takes.

AI-Powered Analysis: ProspectIQ Intelligence uses ensemble machine learning models trained on decades of NBA data. Player projections combine regression analysis, similarity scoring against historical archetypes, and contextual modeling that accounts for team system, coaching style, and roster fit. Trade evaluations run multi-variable optimization across salary cap, positional needs, timeline alignment, and projected win shares.

Key Features

Advanced Analytics

Deep statistical modeling beyond box scores. Player impact metrics, lineup net ratings, clutch performance splits, on/off differentials, and possession-level analysis powered by AI pattern recognition.

Trade Analyzer

Evaluate proposed trades with AI-powered impact analysis. Models salary cap implications across multiple seasons, roster fit scoring, chemistry projections, and comparative value based on historical trade outcomes.

Team Builder

AI-optimized roster construction within salary constraints. Multi-variable optimization considers positional versatility, offensive and defensive scheme fit, age curve projections, and championship probability modeling.

Season Projections

Multi-season player and team projections using ML models. Win probability curves, playoff seeding forecasts, player development trajectories, and age-adjusted performance curves with confidence intervals.

Scouting Network

Comprehensive prospect pipeline from international leagues, NCAA, and G-League. NBA readiness scoring, statistical translation models, archetype matching against historical comparisons, and draft value optimization.

Custom Reports

Generate detailed analytical reports for any player, team, matchup, or trade scenario. Export-ready PDF formats designed for front office presentations, media segments, or fantasy league preparation.

App Screens & How You Interact

Analytics Dashboard

Your Command Center

The dashboard provides a real-time overview of the NBA landscape with AI-curated insights and trending analysis.

  • League Pulse: Daily AI-generated summaries of the most significant statistical trends, breakout performances, and notable shifts in team dynamics across the league.
  • Watchlist: Pin specific players or teams for tracking. See real-time stat updates, projection changes, and alerts when a watched player's trade value shifts significantly.
  • Quick Analysis: Type any player name to instantly see their current stats, projections, trade value, and AI-generated scouting summary. No need to navigate through menus.
  • Trending Insights: AI identifies emerging patterns — players on hot streaks, teams whose underlying metrics diverge from their record, and upcoming schedule difficulty rankings.
Player Analysis

Deep Player Intelligence

Comprehensive player profiles with AI-driven analytics that contextualize performance within team systems and historical archetypes.

  • Impact Metrics: Beyond traditional stats — see offensive and defensive ratings, usage-adjusted efficiency, clutch performance indices, and estimated wins above replacement.
  • Archetype Matching: AI compares the player's statistical profile against thousands of historical NBA careers to identify the closest archetypes and project likely development trajectories.
  • Lineup Context: See how the player performs in different lineup combinations. Identify which teammates unlock the best version of a player and which pairings create redundancy.
  • Injury Risk Model: Workload tracking, minutes distribution analysis, and age-adjusted injury probability based on historical patterns for similar player profiles.
  • Contract Value: Current contract efficiency rating comparing salary to on-court production. Projected market value for upcoming free agents based on comparable signings.
Trade Lab

Evaluate Any Trade Scenario

A sandbox environment where you can construct, evaluate, and compare trade scenarios with AI-powered impact analysis.

  • Build a Trade: Drag players and draft picks between teams. The system automatically validates salary matching rules and flags any CBA compliance issues.
  • Impact Score: Each trade receives an AI-generated impact score for every team involved, projecting win share changes, playoff probability shifts, and championship odds adjustments.
  • Historical Comparisons: The AI identifies similar historical trades and shows how those played out over 1, 3, and 5 years — giving you a reality check on proposed moves.
  • Multi-Team Scenarios: Build three-team and four-team trade scenarios. The optimizer suggests additional pieces that could make a trade work for all parties involved.
  • Save and Share: Save trade scenarios to your library. Share with friends or league-mates to debate the merits of each proposed deal.
Team Builder

Construct Championship Rosters

Build hypothetical rosters from scratch or modify existing teams to explore what-if scenarios.

  • Salary Cap Engine: Real-time cap tracking including luxury tax thresholds, exceptions, and projected future cap holds. The system prevents illegal roster constructions.
  • Optimization Mode: Tell the AI your priorities (maximize defense, build around a specific star, develop young talent) and it suggests the optimal roster construction within your constraints.
  • Chemistry Model: AI estimates lineup chemistry based on play style compatibility, historical on-court synergy data, and role overlap analysis.
  • Simulation: Run your constructed roster through a season simulation to see projected win totals, playoff performance, and championship probability.
Projections Center

Multi-Season Forecasting

Forward-looking analytics that project player development, team trajectories, and league-wide trends.

  • Player Curves: Age-adjusted performance projections showing expected peak, plateau, and decline timelines for any player. Confidence bands show the range of likely outcomes.
  • Team Trajectories: Multi-season win projection graphs for every team, accounting for roster moves, aging, and draft picks. See which teams are trending up or down.
  • Draft Value Models: Project the likely value of future draft picks based on team trajectory, helping evaluate the real cost of trading future assets.
  • Free Agency Previews: Upcoming free agent classes ranked by projected market value, with analysis of which teams have cap space and positional needs.
Scouting Hub

Prospect Pipeline

Track prospects from international leagues, NCAA, and G-League with AI-powered evaluation tools.

  • Prospect Database: Comprehensive profiles for draft-eligible players across all major feeder leagues. Includes physical measurables, statistical production, and AI-generated scouting reports.
  • Translation Models: Statistical translation algorithms that project how college, international, or G-League stats will translate to NBA production — historically the hardest problem in scouting.
  • Big Board: AI-generated draft rankings that update dynamically as new data comes in. See how prospects move up or down based on recent performance.
  • Combine Analytics: When available, combine measurement data is integrated into prospect profiles with positional percentile rankings and historical comparison data.
