AI-powered therapy companion — multi-LLM consensus, neuroplastic adaptation, voice & text sessions, and 24/7 ambient monitoring

What Is Synapta Therapist?

Synapta Therapist is a personal AI therapy companion that provides on-demand emotional support through natural, empathetic conversation. It goes far beyond a simple chatbot — behind every response, a Mitotic Agent Cloner spawns five independent AI clones, each using a different therapeutic strategy. A multi-LLM consensus engine (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, DeepSeek, Llama) selects the best response while a hallucination detection system ensures clinical safety.

Over time, Synapta adapts to you personally through its Neuroplastic Scorer, which uses long-term potentiation (LTP) and long-term depression (LTD) engagement models to learn which therapeutic approaches resonate most with you. A three-phase Resonance Discovery system evolves from exploration to convergence to optimization across your sessions.

Multi-LLM Therapeutic Architecture: Every message you send triggers 5 parallel AI clones across different LLM providers, each applying a different therapeutic lens. The consensus engine evaluates all responses for clinical appropriateness, empathetic quality, and hallucination risk before selecting the single best reply. You see one thoughtful response; behind it, five models competed to give you the best possible support.

Key Features

Therapeutic Conversations

Talk through text or voice with an AI trained in CBT, mindfulness, and reflective listening. The Mitotic Agent Cloner ensures you always receive the most therapeutically appropriate response from 5 competing strategies.

Whisper Companion

24/7 ambient background monitoring that listens to prosodic patterns (volume, pitch, speaking rate, pause patterns) to detect stress. Raw audio is NEVER stored or transmitted — only acoustic features are analyzed on-device.

Voice Sessions

Speak naturally and hear the therapist respond with synthesized voice. Voice input is transcribed to text for processing; the original audio is discarded immediately after transcription for privacy.

Neuroplastic Adaptation

The system learns your therapeutic preferences over time using LTP/LTD engagement modeling. Approaches that help you improve are reinforced; those that do not resonate are gradually deprioritized.

Crisis Detection

Multi-LLM consensus monitors every conversation for crisis signals. If risk is detected, the system provides immediate safety resources, emergency contact notification, and direct routing to 988/Crisis Text Line.

Mood Tracking

Daily mood check-ins with emoji-based logging. Track patterns over time, identify triggers, and see correlations between your mood and session themes or life events.

Multi-Party Sessions

Support for couples therapy sessions where both participants interact with the AI therapist. The system manages turn-taking, tracks each person's perspective, and facilitates constructive dialogue.

Care Coordination

FHIR bridge integration allows you to share session summaries and mood data with your healthcare providers, creating continuity between AI support and professional care.

The Whisper Companion

Whisper is a unique background service that passively monitors your vocal prosody throughout the day. It does not listen to words or record conversations — it only analyzes acoustic features like volume changes, pitch variations, speaking rate, and pause patterns to estimate your stress level.

TierStress LevelIntervention
1Mild elevationSubtle notification with a breathing prompt
2ModerateGentle check-in message asking how you are doing
3ElevatedOffer to start a brief grounding exercise
4HighProactive session invitation with calming guidance
5Crisis-levelSafety resources, emergency contact alert, crisis line routing
Audio Privacy Guarantee: Whisper analyzes prosodic features only. Raw audio is NEVER stored, transmitted, or accessible to anyone — including Alpha Inception. The feature processes audio entirely on your device and immediately discards the raw signal after extracting acoustic measurements. Battery impact is less than 5% per day.

App Screens & How You Interact

Home Screen

Your Daily Starting Point

The Home screen greets you with a mood check-in and quick access to all features. It is designed to take less than five seconds to start a session.

  • Mood Check-In: Tap one of five mood indicators to log how you are feeling. This feeds your mood history and helps the AI personalize session openers.
  • Start Session: A prominent button launches a new therapy session in text or voice mode. Your previous session context is loaded so the therapist remembers what you discussed.
  • Whisper Status: Shows whether the Whisper Companion is active and your current ambient stress level indicator.
  • Homework: Any exercises or reflection prompts assigned in your last session appear here as action cards.
  • Crisis Button: A clearly visible safety button that immediately provides crisis resources, emergency contacts, and direct routing to 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
Therapy Session

Text & Voice Conversations

The core therapeutic interface supports both text and voice input. Behind the scenes, the Mitotic Agent Cloner and multi-LLM consensus work to deliver the most helpful response.

  • Text Mode: A familiar messaging interface where you type freely. The AI responds with thoughtful, empathetic messages that draw on CBT techniques, mindfulness, and reflective listening.
  • Voice Mode: Tap the microphone to speak naturally. Your voice is transcribed in real time, and the therapist responds with synthesized speech. The conversational flow feels more natural than typing.
  • Session Continuity: The therapist remembers context from previous sessions. You can reference past conversations, and the AI builds on themes you have explored together over time.
  • Technique Integration: When the AI identifies an opportunity, it naturally weaves therapeutic techniques into the conversation — cognitive restructuring, grounding, gratitude exercises, or journaling prompts.
  • Session Summary: After each session, you receive a summary card with key themes, any homework assigned, and mood observations. These summaries build a longitudinal record of your therapeutic journey.
Whisper Dashboard

Ambient Stress Monitoring

The Whisper Dashboard shows your prosodic stress patterns over time, helping you understand when and why your stress levels fluctuate.

  • Stress Timeline: A 24-hour chart showing your estimated stress level throughout the day based on vocal prosody analysis. Identify patterns like consistent afternoon stress spikes.
  • Intervention History: See when Whisper sent you a check-in or breathing prompt, and whether you engaged with it.
  • Weekly Patterns: Aggregated view showing which days and times of day tend to have the highest stress, helping you and your therapist identify triggers.
  • Privacy Controls: Granular controls to pause Whisper, set quiet hours, or disable it entirely. Full transparency into exactly what data is and is not being collected.
Mood History

Emotional Patterns Over Time

A visual timeline of your daily mood check-ins, session themes, and Whisper stress data combined into one view.

  • Mood Calendar: A color-coded calendar showing your daily mood at a glance. Tap any day to see session notes and stress data for that date.
  • Trend Lines: Weekly and monthly mood trends with annotations showing when you started or changed therapeutic approaches.
  • Correlation Insights: The AI identifies patterns between mood, session frequency, homework completion, and external factors you have mentioned in sessions.

Who Is Synapta Therapist For?

  • Anyone experiencing stress, anxiety, or low mood who wants on-demand therapeutic support between (or instead of) traditional therapy sessions
  • People on therapy waitlists who need support now while waiting for a human therapist
  • Individuals who prefer text or voice-based communication and find it easier to open up without face-to-face interaction
  • Couples seeking guided communication support through the multi-party session feature
  • Anyone who wants proactive stress monitoring through the Whisper Companion's ambient detection
  • Users aged 17+ — the app is not intended for children or minors under 17
Important Notice: Synapta Therapist is a wellness and self-help tool. It is not a replacement for licensed therapy, psychiatric care, or medical treatment. If you are in crisis or experiencing thoughts of self-harm, please use the in-app Crisis Button, contact 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, or call your local emergency services immediately.

Privacy & Data

Your mental health data deserves the highest level of protection. All conversations are encrypted with AES-256-GCM at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Credentials are stored in iOS Keychain and Android Keystore. Voice audio is transcribed to text and immediately discarded — raw audio is never stored or transmitted. Whisper processes audio entirely on-device.

Your therapy content is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms. LLM providers process your messages under strict data processing agreements that prohibit model training on your data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time — purge completes within 30 days. Inactive accounts (24+ months) are deleted with 30-day advance notice.
