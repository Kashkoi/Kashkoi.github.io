Synapta Therapist

AI-powered therapy companion — supportive conversations, mood tracking & guided wellness exercises

What Is Synapta Therapist?

Synapta Therapist is a personal AI therapy companion that provides on-demand emotional support through natural, empathetic conversation. Whether you are working through anxiety, stress, relationship challenges, or simply need a safe space to process your thoughts, Synapta Therapist is available whenever you need it — no appointments and no waitlists.

The app uses evidence-informed therapeutic techniques including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness practices, and reflective listening to help you reframe negative thinking patterns, explore your emotions, and develop healthier coping strategies over time.

Key Features

Therapeutic Conversations

Talk openly with an AI trained in supportive, evidence-informed techniques. The conversation adapts to your needs, whether you want to vent, problem-solve, or reflect.

Mood Tracking

Log how you are feeling each day with a simple emoji-based check-in. View patterns over time to understand your emotional landscape and identify triggers.

Guided Exercises

Access structured breathing techniques (4-7-8, box breathing), body scan meditations, grounding exercises (5-4-3-2-1), and journaling prompts.

Wellness Streaks

Build consistency with daily check-in streaks. Track your progress and stay motivated with gentle reminders and milestone celebrations.

Sleep Support

Wind-down routines and calming audio designed to help you transition from a busy mind to restful sleep. Guided progressive relaxation included.

Private & Secure

Your conversations are encrypted and your data stays private. No conversations are shared with third parties. You can delete your data at any time.

App Screens & How You Interact

Home Screen

Your Daily Dashboard

When you open Synapta Therapist, the Home screen greets you with a mood check-in prompt and quick-access actions. This is your starting point each day.

  • Mood Check-In: Tap one of five mood indicators (Good, Okay, Low, Anxious, Stressed) to log how you are feeling right now. This takes less than two seconds and feeds into your mood history.
  • Quick Actions: Four large buttons let you jump straight into a therapy session, start a breathing exercise, write a journal entry, or play calming sleep sounds.
  • Wellness Streak: A card at the bottom shows your consecutive days of engagement, encouraging daily check-ins.
Chat Screen

Therapeutic Conversations

The Chat screen is the core of Synapta Therapist. It provides a familiar messaging interface where you can type freely about anything on your mind.

  • Natural Dialogue: The AI responds with thoughtful, empathetic messages. It asks clarifying questions, reflects back what you have said, and offers gentle reframes when appropriate.
  • Technique Suggestions: When the AI identifies that a specific technique might help (like grounding or cognitive restructuring), it offers it naturally within the conversation.
  • Session Continuity: The therapist remembers context within your session so you can explore topics deeply without repeating yourself.
  • Typing Indicator: A calming animation shows when the AI is composing a response, creating a natural conversational rhythm.
Exercises Screen

Guided Wellness Activities

The Exercises screen presents a library of structured mental wellness activities, each with clear time estimates so you can fit them into your schedule.

  • Deep Breathing (5 min): The 4-7-8 technique calms your nervous system with timed inhale, hold, and exhale cycles. Visual guides keep you on pace.
  • Body Scan (10 min): Progressive relaxation from head to toe. Audio guidance helps you notice and release tension in each muscle group.
  • Grounding — 5-4-3-2-1 (3 min): Reconnect with the present moment using your five senses. The app walks you through noticing 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you touch, 2 you smell, and 1 you taste.
  • Gratitude Reflection (5 min): Guided journaling that shifts your focus to positive aspects of your day. Research shows daily gratitude practice reduces anxiety and improves sleep.
  • Sleep Prep (15 min): A full wind-down routine combining breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, and visualization to prepare your mind and body for rest.
  • Mindful Walk (10 min): Transform a simple walk into a mindfulness practice with audio cues that draw your attention to your surroundings and body.
Journal Screen

Reflective Writing

The Journal provides a private space for freeform writing. Studies show that expressive writing reduces stress and helps process difficult emotions.

  • Free Writing: Open a blank entry and write whatever comes to mind. No prompts required.
  • Guided Prompts: Prefer structure? Choose from prompts like "What am I grateful for today?", "What is weighing on me?", or "What would I tell a friend in my situation?"
  • Entry History: Review past entries to reflect on your journey and see how your thinking has evolved over time.

How It Works

1

Open the App & Check In

Start your day by tapping a mood indicator on the Home screen. This takes two seconds and helps you build self-awareness around your emotional patterns.

2

Start a Conversation

Tap "Start a Session" and begin talking about whatever is on your mind. The AI listens, reflects, and guides you with evidence-based therapeutic techniques.

3

Try a Guided Exercise

When you need something more structured, browse the Exercises library. Choose an activity that fits your time and current needs — from 3-minute grounding to 15-minute sleep prep.

4

Track Your Progress

Over time, your mood history reveals patterns. The wellness streak keeps you motivated, and journal entries let you look back and see growth.

Who Is Synapta Therapist For?

  • Anyone experiencing stress, anxiety, or low mood who wants on-demand support between (or instead of) traditional therapy sessions
  • People who want to build mental wellness habits like daily mood tracking, journaling, and mindfulness
  • Those on therapy waitlists who need support now while waiting for a human therapist
  • Individuals who prefer text-based communication and find it easier to open up in writing
  • Anyone curious about therapy who wants a low-pressure way to explore their thoughts and feelings
Important Notice: Synapta Therapist is a wellness and self-help tool. It is not a replacement for licensed therapy, psychiatric care, or medical treatment. If you are in crisis or experiencing thoughts of self-harm, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately.
