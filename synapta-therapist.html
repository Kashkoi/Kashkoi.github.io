What Is Synapta Therapist?

Synapta Therapist is a comprehensive AI therapy companion with 57 feature modules that provides on-demand emotional support through natural, empathetic conversation. It operates in three distinct therapy modes — Autonomous (AI-only), Assistant (AI collaborating with a human therapist), and Hybrid (supervised AI with therapist oversight) — giving users and clinicians full control over the level of human involvement.

Behind every response, a Mitotic Agent Cloner spawns five independent AI clones, each using a different therapeutic strategy. A multi-LLM consensus engine (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, DeepSeek, Llama) selects the best response while a hallucination detection system ensures clinical safety. Over time, Synapta adapts to you personally through its Neuroplastic Scorer, which uses long-term potentiation (LTP) and long-term depression (LTD) engagement models to learn which therapeutic approaches resonate most with you.

A 16-step Intelligent Therapy Orchestration pipeline powers each interaction — incorporating treatment protocols, playbooks, difficult-moment handlers, a microskills library, case conceptualization, internal supervisor validation, a treatment planning engine, session memory with a learning engine, a Chain of Empathy cognitive pipeline, a Virtual Context Manager, and HyDE-based semantic retrieval for deep contextual understanding.

Multi-LLM Therapeutic Architecture: Every message you send triggers 5 parallel AI clones across different LLM providers, each applying a different therapeutic lens. The consensus engine evaluates all responses for clinical appropriateness, empathetic quality, and hallucination risk before selecting the single best reply. You see one thoughtful response; behind it, five models competed to give you the best possible support.
Device Abstraction Layer (DAL): Orchestrates 24+ sensor types across multiple modalities with a Dual-Embodiment architecture — Consumer Tier (OS health APIs) and Clinical Tier (direct HAL-to-TEE). Sensors include HR, HRV, SpO2, respiratory rate, blood pressure, EDA (skin conductance), accelerometer, gyroscope, skin temperature, GPS, ambient light, barometer, microphone prosody, EEG (Bluetooth headbands), CGM (glucose), and EMG. A five-level Graceful Degradation Protocol (Full, Reduced, Sparse, User-Initiated, Offline) ensures the app continues functioning regardless of available hardware.

The Whisper Companion

Whisper is a unique background service that passively monitors your vocal prosody throughout the day. It does not listen to words or record conversations — it only analyzes acoustic features like volume changes, pitch variations, speaking rate, and pause patterns to estimate your stress level. At Tier 4, Whisper Therapy activates mini check-in sessions — quick 2–5 minute micro-interventions with mood emoji scales, coping exercises, and escalation options.

TierStress LevelIntervention
1Mild elevationSubtle notification with a breathing prompt
2ModerateGentle check-in message asking how you are doing
3ElevatedOffer to start a brief grounding exercise
4HighWhisper Therapy: 2–5 minute micro-session with mood scale, coping exercises (breathing/grounding), and escalation options
5Crisis-levelSafety resources, emergency contact alert, crisis line routing
Audio Privacy Guarantee: Whisper analyzes prosodic features only. Raw audio is NEVER stored, transmitted, or accessible to anyone — including Alpha Inception. The feature processes audio entirely on your device and immediately discards the raw signal after extracting acoustic measurements. DAL sensor data in Consumer Tier is processed locally on-device. Battery impact is less than 5% per day.

Device Abstraction Layer (DAL)

The DAL orchestrates 24+ sensor types from multiple modalities through a Dual-Embodiment architecture. The Consumer Tier integrates with OS health APIs (Apple HealthKit, Google Health Connect) for everyday use, while the Clinical Tier provides direct HAL-to-TEE (Hardware Abstraction Layer to Trusted Execution Environment) connectivity for medical-grade sensor data in clinical settings.

Cardiovascular Sensors

Heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), SpO2 (blood oxygen), respiratory rate, and blood pressure monitoring from wearable devices and phone sensors.

Electrodermal & Motion

EDA (skin conductance) for stress response, accelerometer and gyroscope for activity and sleep patterns, and skin temperature for physiological state tracking.

Environmental & Location

GPS for context-aware interventions, ambient light for circadian rhythm analysis, and barometer for environmental pressure changes that may affect mood.

Advanced Biometrics

Microphone prosody analysis, EEG from Bluetooth headbands for neurofeedback, CGM (continuous glucose monitoring), and EMG for muscle tension tracking.

Graceful Degradation Protocol (5 levels): Full (all sensors available) → Reduced (primary sensors only) → Sparse (phone sensors only) → User-Initiated (manual input only) → Offline (cached models, no connectivity). The app provides a complete therapeutic experience at every level.

Specialized response strategies for therapeutic ruptures, emotional flooding, resistance, and other challenging moments that require careful clinical navigation.

Microskills Library

A comprehensive library of therapeutic microskills — reflections, reframes, open questions, affirmations, summaries — deployed contextually within conversations.

Case Conceptualization

Dynamic case formulation that builds and updates a clinical understanding of your patterns, triggers, strengths, and therapeutic needs across sessions.

Internal Supervisor

A validation layer that reviews every proposed response against clinical best practices, ethical guidelines, and safety protocols before delivery.

Chain of Empathy

A cognitive pipeline that processes your input through perspective-taking, emotional attunement, and compassionate reasoning before generating a response.

Session Memory Engine

Persistent session memory with a learning engine that tracks therapeutic themes, breakthroughs, setbacks, and relationship patterns across your entire history.

HyDE Semantic Retrieval

Hypothetical Document Embedding retrieval that generates ideal therapeutic responses first, then searches for the most relevant clinical knowledge to ground and validate them.

Privacy & Data

Your mental health data deserves the highest level of protection. All conversations are encrypted with AES-256-GCM at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Credentials are stored in iOS Keychain and Android Keystore. Voice audio is transcribed to text and immediately discarded — raw audio is never stored or transmitted. Whisper processes audio entirely on-device. DAL sensor data in Consumer Tier is processed locally on your device.

Your therapy content is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms. LLM providers process your messages under strict data processing agreements (DPAs) that prohibit model training on your data. The Privacy Dashboard provides full audit logs showing exactly what data exists, who has accessed it, and when. Permissions Management gives you granular consent controls over every data source.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time — purge completes within 30 days. Inactive accounts (24+ months) are deleted with 30-day advance notice.
