Synapta AI Therapist

Patent-pending multi-LLM therapeutic AI platform with real-time physiological monitoring

Overview

Synapta is a patent-pending AI therapeutic platform that combines multiple large language models with real-time physiological monitoring to deliver personalized, evidence-based mental health support. The platform features always-on crisis prevention, automated clinical billing compliance, cross-device session continuity, and voice-interactive therapy sessions with customizable therapist personas.

Unlike traditional telehealth apps, Synapta uses a multi-agent architecture where specialized AI subsystems handle crisis detection, care coordination, treatment planning, and regulatory compliance simultaneously — ensuring comprehensive therapeutic support while maintaining clinical-grade audit trails.

Key Features

Crisis Prevention (Always-On)

Life-saving background service with suicide prevention, overdose detection, violence de-escalation, self-harm interruption, and psychotic episode early warning. Integrated with 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

Voice-Interactive Therapy

Choose from 4 therapist voices (2 female, 2 male) and name your AI therapist for a personalized experience. Full speech-to-text and text-to-speech for natural conversation.

RTM Billing Compliance

Automated CPT code tracking (98975-98981) with cryptographically verified engagement metrics, predictive audit defense, and audit-ready claim generation for clinic administrators.

Cross-Device Continuity

Seamless session handoff between devices with encrypted state transfer, sensor recalibration, and conflict-free replicated data types (CRDTs) for offline resilience.

Care Coordination

Multi-provider care team management with secure messaging, treatment plan synchronization, and automated referral workflows.

LLM Morphing

Dynamic switching between AI models based on therapeutic context — using the optimal model for each clinical scenario while maintaining conversation continuity.

Physiological Monitoring

Real-time biometric integration tracking heart rate, HRV, EDA, skin temperature, sleep patterns, and movement for objective treatment progress measurement.

Agent Governance

Transparent AI decision-making with full audit trails, configurable authority schedules, and break-confidence protocols for crisis situations.

How It Works

Synapta operates as a multi-agent system where specialized AI subsystems work together:

  • The Crisis Prevention Engine continuously monitors physiological baselines and detects dangerous deviations using z-score analysis, including paradoxical calming patterns that indicate imminent suicide risk
  • The Therapeutic Core manages your treatment plan, adapting interventions based on real-time biometric data and session history
  • The Billing Engine automatically tracks engagement for Remote Therapeutic Monitoring (RTM) CPT codes, ensuring your clinician's practice stays compliant
  • Cross-device sync maintains your therapeutic state across phones, tablets, and other devices with military-grade encryption

All interactions are protected by SHA-256 hash-chained audit trails that ensure data integrity and regulatory compliance.
